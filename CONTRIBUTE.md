# Contributing to Nebula

## Introduction
Thank you for your interest in Nebula. While this project is still in its early stages, contributions are welcome. This guide will help you get started.

## How to Contribute
Currently, the main areas for contribution are:
- Bug reporting
- Suggestions for features or improvements

## Bug Reporting
If you encounter a bug, please open an issue on the GitHub repository with a clear description of the problem. Include steps to reproduce the bug, and any relevant screenshots or error messages.

## Feature Requests and Improvements
Have an idea to make Nebula better? Feel free to open an issue on the repository to discuss new features or improvements; or head over to the discussions tab of the repository.

## Questions and Discussions
For any questions or discussions, you can join our Discord server: https://discord.gg/TA3WnjGMC8

Or email me at: setoichi.dev@gmail.com

Your interest and support are greatly appreciated, and I look forward to growing Nebula with the community.
